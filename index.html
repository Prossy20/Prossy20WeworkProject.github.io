The
Wework project

In this project, we came up with a mobile app called the wework app which needs a person that wants to advertise a job to first register then login and then post a job. Upon posting the job one should fill in details like job type, the amount to be paid after the job has been done, what time it needs to be done and location of the job. On the otherhand, one whose in search of jobs needs to be registered and verified as an agent for security reasons thereafter can be able to view and apply for the available jobs. Those qualified as agents can also receive notifications as whether they have been rejected by the person giving the job or accepted.
